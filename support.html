Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I restore my purchase?

Go to the Settings menu or the "Remove Ads" screen within the game and tap the "Restore Purchases" button.

2. The game freezes or crashes.

Please try closing the app completely and reopening it. If the problem persists, ensure you have the latest version updated from the App Store.

3. How do I turn off the sound?

You can toggle sound and vibration settings from the main menu.
